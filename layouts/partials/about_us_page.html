About us

We aim to become a platform where candidates can find the role which best suits their profile and interest. This platform will facilitate the human connection and try to match the opportunities with skills.

Our future plan

At this stage, we are just a job curation platform. We plan to become the platform which allows the candidates to browse through a list of opportunities and find the best match.

We aim to create a platform with human interactions in mind - where a company and candidate both can find a good match. This platform will be focused for early-stage companies where we focus on simple question & answer model of connection.

We hope to make the MVP for the same live in the mid of this year and then iterate on the same to achieve the goal.
